Xenoblade Chronicles Review

Xenoblade Chronicles, developed by Monolith Soft from Nintendo in 2010/2011, is one of the best video games I’ve ever played. It’s an epic JRPG that follows the journey of a young man named Shulk who tries to solve the mystery of a legendary sword called The Monado. On his way he encounters new friends and foes and finds out that The Monado is much more than an ordinary sword. This game looks beautiful, even if this is a Wii game. The different environments are varied, huge and colourful. The biggest problem about the visuals though are when you get close up to the characers. They aren’t ugly per say, but they are pretty blurry and doesn't have the best animations (eating looks horrible).
